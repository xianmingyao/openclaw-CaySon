```
[想法/灵感]

    ↓
[需求分析] → requirements-analysis, context-product-manager
    ↓
[竞品分析] → competitive-analysis, competitor-teardown
    ↓
[产品策略] → product-strategy, CPO
    ↓
[PRD写作] → prd-writer
    ↓
[原型设计] → wireframe, prd-to-prototype
    ↓
[MVP验证] → mvp, feature-requirements-clarification

[用户画像] → customer-persona
```
